New Zealand Skilled Migration

If you would like to settle and work in New Zealand, and your skills are currently in demand by the New Zealand government, you may apply for a New Zealand Skilled Migration visa. Assessment will be based on your age, education, work experience, English language proficiency and links with New Zealand.

GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

  • • Aged below 55 at the time of application
  • • Occupation is in demand as per New Zealand's occupation list
  • • English proficiency as required
  • • At least four years of the basic level qualification
  • • Able to demonstrate past work experience
  • • Provide evidence of proficient English or higher (if secondary)
  • • Meet health and character requirements
