Canada also grants Visa to Immigrants through various provincial nominee programs. The applications are assessed on applicant's ability to establish in particular province while immigrating to Canada. Each province and territory has its own streams and criteria for their PNP.

FOUR TYPES OF
CANADIAN PROVINCIAL
NOMINEE PROGRAMS
(PNP)

  • • The individuals who hold a valid work permit and is recently or currently, employed in a province.
  • • Candidates who qualify for an existing PNP program and submit an expression of Interest to the Express Entry pool. However, there are certain kinds of PNP programs which chooses the potential candidates from the Federal express entry slot on the basis of their academic qualification or skilled work experience.
  • • An applicant who possesses some kind of connection with the province they are applying for. For instance, it can be for someone who studied in a province or earned a job offer from an employer in the province.
  • • On certain conditions, PNP program allows the candidate to sponsor a family member. However, to eligible for this, the applicant should meet some necessary requirements.

ALBERTA

AINP

Alberta Immigrant Nominee Program offers pathways for skilled workers, international graduates, and entrepreneurs.

BRITISH COLUMBIA

BC PNP

BC Provincial Nominee Program for skilled workers, healthcare professionals, and international graduates.

MANITOBA

MPNP

Manitoba Provincial Nominee Program for skilled workers with job offers and international education stream.

NEW BRUNSWICK

NBPNP

New Brunswick Provincial Nominee Program for skilled workers and entrepreneurs with French language priority.

ONTARIO

OINP

Ontario Immigrant Nominee Program for skilled workers, international students, and French-speaking skilled workers.

SASKATCHEWAN

SINP

Saskatchewan Immigrant Nominee Program for in-demand occupations and international skilled workers.

NEWFOUNDLAND & LABRADOR

NLPNP

Newfoundland and Labrador Provincial Nominee Program for skilled workers and international graduates.

NOVA SCOTIA

NSNP

Nova Scotia Nominee Program for skilled workers, international graduates, and entrepreneurs.

PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND

PEI PNP

Prince Edward Island Provincial Nominee Program for skilled workers and business investors.

YUKON

YNP

Yukon Nominee Program for skilled workers with job offers and critical impact workers.

NORTHWEST TERRITORIES

NTNP

Northwest Territories Nominee Program for skilled workers and business investors with territorial connections.
