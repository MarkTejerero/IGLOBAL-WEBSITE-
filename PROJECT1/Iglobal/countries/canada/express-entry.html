The Express Entry

The Express Entry program is designed to allow skilled workers from around the world to apply for immigration to Canada. Skilled immigrants are chosen as permanent residents based on their ability to settle in Canada and contribute to the growth of the economy.

Using a point system, an applicant is assessed under the federal skilled worker class according to various factors that will indicate whether there is a strong likelihood that the applicant and dependants will successfully establish in Canada. Ideal applicants under this class are persons who have been working in managerial, professional or technical occupations "open" to prospective immigrants to Canada. The selection rules particularly favor applicants with government approved job offers in Canada.

GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

  • • Have at least one year of continuous full-time or equivalent part-time work experience
  • • Classified at skill levels A, B or O
  • • Skilled work experience and a full-time permanent employment opportunity
  • • Arranged employment (if you don't already have it arranged) approved by HRSDC
  • • Have completed a PhD in Canada, or have substantial experience in Canada
  • • Substantial amounts of study in Canada
  • • Have at least one year of experience in Canada
  • • Pass a minimum functional level of language ability for one of Canada's two official languages (English or French)

ADVANTAGES

  • ✓ Free primary and secondary education in and subsidized tertiary education
  • ✓ Healthcare benefits
  • ✓ Relatively easy requirements due to a wider array of eligible occupations
  • ✓ Ease of travel to the United States of America
  • ✓ Pathway to Canadian citizenship
